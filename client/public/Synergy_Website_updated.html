The Process

Simple. Clean.
Sustainable.

Four easy steps — your school does almost nothing. We handle pickup, weighing, and recycling at zero cost.

01
🤝
Partnership
We visit your school, explain the program, and sign a simple agreement. No fees, no obligations.
02
📦
Collection Point
Your school sets aside a dry spot. Waste paper — books, printed pages — is stored as it accumulates.
03
🚚
We Pick Up
Synergy arrives on schedule, weighs the paper, and handles all transport from your location. Zero hassle.
04
♻️
Recycled Right
Paper goes to certified recyclers and is transformed into new products, closing the loop on waste.
